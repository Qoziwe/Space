THE CLOSEST PLANET TO THE SUN

Mercury

Mercury is the smallest and innermost planet in our solar system. It's a rocky planet, much like Earth, but it's also the fastest, zipping around the sun in just 88 Earth days. Mercury is named after the Roman messenger god, who was known for his speed.

It's a scorching hot world, with temperatures reaching over 800 degrees Fahrenheit during the day, but plummeting to hundreds of degrees below zero at night. It's a fascinating place, with a cratered surface, a thin atmosphere, and a magnetic field that's only about 1% as strong as Earth's.
